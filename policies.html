Policies

Financing

Payment must be made at the time of purchase. We convinently accept cash, personal checks, Discover, American Express, Visa, and Mastercard.

Deliveries

Orders that are placed Monday through Friday are shipped one business day after order has been placed. Any orders placed on the weekends or holidays will be shipped on the following Monday. After the package has been shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. On average, it takes 2-7 business days to receive packages.

Returns

Customers may return the product 30 days after they have purchased it. The only question we will ask is why did the product not work out.
