About Us

Our History

Durham House of Pets was founded by Hannah in 1985. When the company first opened, it was selling what many expect to find at a petstore. Now we sell items that differentiate us as a company, like our water and food bowls that alert the owner via text message that they need to be refilled. Over the past 33 years, we have became more than just your average pet store. We are a one of a kind experience with our customizable products and excellent customer service.

Why DHP?

Durham House of Pets strives to provide a specialized experience for every customer. We take suggestions from customers about products we should supply or ones we should create.We even have the option of helping create a product that suits your needs the best! If a customer purchases a product that is not right for their pet they have the option to return it, no questions asked. Our employees all have a passion for animals and they all have a ton of pets! They are educated on what would be the best product for a particular situation from training as well as personal experiences. We are conveniently located at The University of New Hampshire in Durham, NH.
